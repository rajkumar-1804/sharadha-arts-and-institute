Welcome to Sharadha Arts And Institute

Tanjore paintings, originating from the town of Tanjore in South India, are renowned for their vibrant colors, intricate designs, and the use of gold foil. This traditional art form dates back to the 16th century and often depicts Hindu deities, showcasing rich textures and elaborate embellishments.







Key Features:

Materials: Crafted with 22k gold foil, semi-precious stones, and natural colors.

Techniques: Utilizes a gesso technique for a raised effect, enhancing visual depth.

Themes: Commonly features gods and goddesses, reflecting deep cultural significance.
