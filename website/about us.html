About Us

Shradha Arts and Institute of Tanjore is dedicated to preserving and promoting traditional Indian art forms, specializing in fabric and glass paintings. Our expert courses teach vibrant techniques in fabric painting, and the delicate art of glass painting that creates stunning light-reflecting masterpieces.



We provide a creative environment for artists of all levels, offering workshops and hands-on training to help students develop their skills and connect with India's rich artistic heritage. Whether you're starting your journey or refining your craft, Shradha Arts is your gateway to mastering these timeless art forms.
