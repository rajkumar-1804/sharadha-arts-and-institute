Courses Available

Tanjore Paintings

Tanjore paintings are characterized by their rich and vivid colors, glittering gold foils, and inlay of glass beads and precious gems. These panel paintings are typically done on wooden planks.

Fabric Paintings

Fabric paintings are created on various textiles, allowing for beautiful designs on items like blouses, sarees, and home decor. This art form combines creativity with functionality.

Glass Paintings

Glass painting is a captivating art form where paint is applied to glass surfaces to create beautiful, decorative images. The transparency of glass enhances the vibrancy of the colors used.
