Welcome to Sharadha Arts And Institute

Fabric paintings are a vibrant and versatile art form that involves applying paint to fabric surfaces, transforming ordinary textiles into stunning works of art. This technique allows artists to express their creativity while creating functional and decorative pieces.







Key Features:

Techniques: Artists use methods like brush painting, stenciling, and screen printing to create unique designs.

Materials: High-quality fabric paints and a variety of fabrics, such as cotton and silk, are used for durability and vibrancy.
